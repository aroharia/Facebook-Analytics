Why Fetchbook?

Data empowers people to improve their lives

Facebook has approximately 2.07 billion number of users. With the emergence of social media, people today are constantly trying to portray themselves on social media. With all the information out their on Facebook, it can be a little overwhelming. Some users might want to see their most popular post or which one of their friends comment on their picture the most. Allowing the user to analyze whatever small component of Facebook that they desire, allows them to better portray themselves on Social Media as well as look through information in a more efficient, effective way.
